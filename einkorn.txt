WHAT IS EINKORN?
------------------
- Ancient wheat variety, never hybridized
- Different gluten structure than modern wheat
- Some people with wheat sensitivity tolerate it better
- Higher protein and mineral content than modern wheat

COOKING WITH EINKORN
----------------------
- Absorbs less water than modern flour
- Reduce liquid by 15-20% in recipes
- Mix less — the gluten is more delicate
- Works well for pasta, flatbreads, pancakes, and cookies

WHERE TO FIND IT
------------------
- Health food stores and specialty grocers
- Online retailers
- Look for "einkorn" specifically (not spelt or emmer)
