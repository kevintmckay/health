STARTING POINT
Deconditioned, gets winded fast, sciatica risk
GOAL: 30-45 min continuous walking most days

BEFORE START
Supportive shoes with good cushioning (replace if worn)
Flat, even surfaces to start (sidewalks, tracks, malls)
Avoid hills/uneven terrain until week 6+
Pace: can hold conversation but feel slightly winded
Stop immediately if sharp pain or radiating leg symptoms

PHASE 1: BUILD HABIT (WEEKS 1-4)

Week 1: Mon 10, Tue rest, Wed 10, Thu rest, Fri 10, Sat 10, Sun rest (4 days, longest 10 min)
Week 2: Mon 12, Tue rest, Wed 12, Thu rest, Fri 12, Sat 15, Sun rest (4 days, longest 15 min)
Week 3: Mon 15, Tue rest, Wed 15, Thu 10, Fri 15, Sat 15, Sun rest (5 days, longest 15 min)
Week 4: Mon 15, Tue 10, Wed 15, Thu rest, Fri 15, Sat 20, Sun rest (5 days, longest 20 min)

PHASE 2: BUILD DURATION (WEEKS 5-8)

Week 5: Mon 20, Tue 10, Wed 20, Thu rest, Fri 20, Sat 20, Sun rest (5 days, longest 20 min)
Week 6: Mon 20, Tue 15, Wed 20, Thu rest, Fri 20, Sat 25, Sun rest (5 days, longest 25 min)
Week 7: Mon 25, Tue 15, Wed 25, Thu rest, Fri 25, Sat 25, Sun rest (5 days, longest 25 min)
Week 8: Mon 25, Tue 15, Wed 25, Thu 15, Fri 25, Sat 30, Sun rest (6 days, longest 30 min)

PHASE 3: BUILD CONSISTENCY (WEEKS 9-12)

Week 9: Mon 30, Tue 15, Wed 30, Thu 15, Fri 30, Sat 30, Sun rest (6 days, longest 30 min)
Week 10: Mon 30, Tue 20, Wed 30, Thu 20, Fri 30, Sat 35, Sun rest (6 days, longest 35 min)
Week 11: Mon 30, Tue 20, Wed 30, Thu 20, Fri 30, Sat 35, Sun rest or easy 15 (6 days, longest 35 min)
Week 12: Mon 30, Tue 20, Wed 35, Thu 20, Fri 30, Sat 40, Sun rest or easy 15 (6 days, longest 40 min)

AFTER 12 WEEKS
Maintain 30-45 min walks 5-6 days/week
Start adding gentle hills/inclines if sciatica allows
Increase pace gradually rather than adding more time
Consider longer walk on weekends (45-60 min)

SCIATICA MANAGEMENT DURING WALKS
If symptoms start, stop and do standing back extension (hands on hips, gently lean back, hold 5 sec, repeat 3-5x)
Shorten next walk by 5 min and rebuild
Avoid walking through radiating leg pain (mild back tightness ok, nerve pain not)
If flare-up, take 1-2 rest days then resume at shorter duration
Don't restart entire plan, just drop back a week and continue

TIPS
Same time every day builds habit fastest (morning best for most)
Bad weather: mall, big box store, treadmill
Bring phone for music/podcasts (makes time pass faster)
Track walks (phone app, notebook, calendar check marks)
Missed day not failure, just walk next day
Soreness normal, sharp pain not (know difference)
