MORNING
Wake up (same time daily, including weekends)
Get outside 10 min daylight (resets circadian rhythm)
Meditation (3-15 min depending on week)
Breakfast (balanced Mediterranean meal, at table)
Morning walk (per walking plan)

MIDDAY
Lunch (protein + veg + small starch, last caffeine before this)
Exercise session (scheduled days):
  Routine A: McGill Big 3 + hip exercises (15-20 min)
  Routine B: Resistance exercises (10-15 min)
Stretches at end when muscles warm

AFTERNOON (HIGHEST RISK 2-5 PM)
Weeks 1-2: Just notice, log in food/mood journal
Weeks 3-4: Catch the thought, write it down
Weeks 5+: Full toolkit:
  1. Pause, don't go to kitchen
  2. RAIN check (Recognize, Allow, Investigate, Non-identify)
  3. Ask: physical hunger or emotional?
  4. If emotional: 15-min timer, do alternative (walk, water, call, task)
  5. If physical: planned protein snack
  6. Log it
Snack if needed: Greek yogurt, nuts, fruit, hummus with vegetables

EVENING
Dinner (protein + veg + starch, at table)
After dinner: light activity ok (easy walk, stretching)
Kitchen closes (eg 8 PM, brush teeth)
If urge hits: tea, water, 4-7-8 breathing

BEFORE BED
Wind-down 30-60 min before bedtime:
  Dim lights, screens off/night mode
  No news/work email/stress
15-30 min before bed:
  Light stretching, read book in chair, warm shower
Lights out (same time nightly)
  Phone face down/another room
  Pillow under knees (back) or between knees (side)
  If not asleep in 20 min, get up, sit in dim light until drowsy

CBT CHECK-IN (2 MIN DAILY)
Did I eat outside planned meals? Y/N
If yes: What feeling? What thought? What trigger?
What went well today?

PHASED ROLLOUT
Week 1: Wake/sleep times, daylight 10 min, walk 4 days, diet plan, meditation 3 min, food/mood log
Week 2: Continue, kitchen closes rule starts
Week 3: Add Routine A 3x/week, walk 5 days, CBT catch thoughts, meditation 5 min + body scan
Week 4: Add Routine B 2x/week, walk 5 days, meditation 7 min
Week 5: CBT challenge/reframe thoughts, meditation 10 min
Week 6+: Full routine, CBT urge surfing, continue progressing

ACCOUNTABILITY
Tell one person: friend, partner, family, walking partner, doctor, PT, online community
Daily text: "walked 15 min today"

IF DERAILED
Bad day: do next thing on schedule
Bad week: pick back up where left off
Sciatica flare: see flare-up-protocol.txt
