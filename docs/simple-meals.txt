SIMPLE MEALS
Each meal: minimal steps, minimal cleanup

BREAKFAST (pick one)
  Oats + yogurt + berries
    1/2 cup oats, cook. Top with 1 cup Greek yogurt, handful blueberries, walnuts.

  Egg + bread + fruit
    1 egg scrambled in EVOO. Einkorn toast. Apple or orange on the side.

  Cottage cheese bowl
    1 cup cottage cheese, strawberries, almonds, drizzle EVOO.

LUNCH (pick one)
  Big salad
    Mixed greens + canned chickpeas (drained) + tomato + cucumber + feta.
    Dress with EVOO + lemon.

  Lentil bowl
    Heat canned lentils. Serve over bulgur or barley. Add spinach, squeeze lemon, EVOO.

  Bean + veg plate
    Heat canned black beans with garlic. Side of raw carrots, peppers, celery.
    Einkorn bread if hungry.

  Cabbage + chickpea saute
    Chop cabbage, saute in EVOO with garlic. Add drained chickpeas, heat through.
    Squeeze lemon, pinch oregano.

DINNER (pick one)
  Salmon + broccoli
    Salmon fillet in EVOO, salt, pepper. Bake 400F 12 min.
    Broccoli on same pan, toss in EVOO. Done.

  Cod + cabbage
    Cod fillet, salt, pepper, lemon. Bake 400F 10 min.
    Shred cabbage, saute in EVOO with garlic until soft.

  Chicken + greens + grain
    Chicken breast in EVOO, salt, oregano. Pan sear 6 min per side.
    Serve over cooked barley with handful of spinach stirred in.

  Shrimp + greens
    Shrimp in EVOO with garlic, salt, pepper. Saute 3 min per side.
    Serve over spinach or mixed greens with lemon and EVOO.

  Tofu + Brussels sprouts
    Cube tofu, pan fry in EVOO until golden.
    Halve Brussels sprouts, roast 400F 20 min with EVOO.
    Squeeze lemon over both.

  Pasta + lentils + kale
    Cook whole wheat pasta. Heat canned lentils.
    Wilt kale in EVOO + garlic. Toss together. Lemon. Feta if you want.

  Tempeh + sweet potato
    Slice tempeh, pan fry in EVOO until crispy.
    Microwave sweet potato. Split, drizzle EVOO, salt.
    Side of raw carrots or salad greens.

SNACKS
  Apple + walnuts
  Greek yogurt + berries
  Cottage cheese + figs
  Raw veg + hummus (carrots, celery, peppers, cabbage wedges)

NOTES
  EVOO on everything.
  Canned beans and lentils save the most time -- just drain and heat.
  Cook grains in bulk once, refrigerate, use all week.
  Every meal: protein + veg + grain. Keep it boring. Boring works.
