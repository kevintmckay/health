MEDITERRANEAN PRINCIPLES
Fatty fish 2-3x/week (salmon primary, cod for variety)
Colorful vegetables daily (variety of 5+ types)
Extra virgin olive oil as primary fat (2-3 tbsp/day)
Walnuts, almonds (1/4 cup daily)
Whole grains daily (oats, barley, bulgur, whole wheat)
Legumes 3-4x/week minimum
Fruit 2-3 servings daily (varied: berries, citrus, apples, figs)
Fresh herbs liberally (oregano, basil, rosemary, thyme, parsley)
Turmeric, ginger
Fermented dairy (yogurt, small amounts of cheese)
Red meat no more than 2x/month
Poultry 2-3x/week
Eggs max 1/day
Avoid: seed oils, fried foods, processed meats, excess sugar, alcohol
