MCGILL BIG 3 - CORE STABILITY

1. CURL-UP
Lie on back
One knee bent, other leg straight
Hands under lower back (locks spine neutral)
Lift head/shoulders 1 inch off floor (not a crunch)
Lower back doesn't move, hands prevent this
Hold 6-8 sec
Switch bent leg halfway through sets
Sets: 3x6 reps (6-8 sec holds)

2. SIDE PLANK
Lie on side, elbow under shoulder
Knees bent 90° (modified version)
Lift hips to form straight line from knees to shoulders
Hold 6-8 sec
Don't let hips sag or rotate
Progression: modified (knees) → full (feet)
Sets: 3x6 reps each side (6-8 sec holds)

3. BIRD-DOG
Hands and knees, back flat
Extend right arm forward + left leg back simultaneously
Arm and leg in line with body, not raised above
Hold 6-8 sec
ZERO torso movement
Make fist with extended hand
Sweep floor on return, don't drop
Sets: 3x6 reps each side (6-8 sec holds)

HIP EXERCISES - MOBILITY AND STRENGTH

4. GLUTE BRIDGE
Lie on back, knees bent, feet flat hip-width
Squeeze glutes, lift hips to straight line knees-to-shoulders
Hold 3-5 sec at top
Lower slowly
Drive through heels
Don't arch lower back at top
Should feel in glutes, not lower back
Sets: 3x10 reps (3-5 sec hold)

5. CLAMSHELL
Lie on side, knees bent 45°, hips stacked
Feet together
Raise top knee without letting hips roll back
Hold 2-3 sec at top
Lower slowly
Feel in side/back of hip (gluteus medius)
Add resistance band above knees to increase difficulty
Sets: 3x12 reps each side

6. HIP FLEXOR STRETCH
Kneel on one knee (pillow under knee)
Other foot flat, knee 90°
Shift weight forward over front foot
Feel stretch in front of hip on kneeling side
Hold 30 sec
Don't arch lower back, tuck pelvis slightly
Squeeze glute on kneeling side to deepen
Sets: 2x30 sec each side

7. PIRIFORMIS STRETCH
Lie on back, knees bent, feet flat
Cross right ankle over left knee (figure-4)
Grab behind left thigh (or use towel)
Pull left thigh toward chest
Feel deep stretch in right glute/hip
Hold 30 sec
Use towel if flexibility limited
Sets: 2x30 sec each side

RESISTANCE TRAINING - MUSCLE PRESERVATION

8. WALL PUSH-UP
Stand arm's length from wall
Palms on wall shoulder height, shoulder-width apart
Bend elbows, lean toward wall
Touch chest/nose to wall
Push back to start
Keep body straight line
Progression: wall → counter/table → knee → full push-up
Sets: 3x10-15 reps

9. CHAIR-ASSISTED SQUAT
Stand facing away from sturdy chair
Feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out
Arms extended for balance
Lower toward chair, touch lightly with glutes
Stand by driving through heels
Weight in heels, not toes
Knees track over toes, don't cave inward
Chest up, back neutral
Chair is safety net, not seat
Progression: high chair → standard chair → no chair
Sets: 3x8-12 reps

10. RESISTANCE BAND ROW
Sit in chair, band around doorknob chest height
Hold one end each hand, arms extended
Light tension at start
Pull band toward ribcage, squeeze shoulder blades
Hold 2 sec at back
Slowly return
Elbows close to body
Torso stays still
No band: use towel looped around doorknob, hold 10 sec
Sets: 3x12-15 reps

PROGRAMMING

ROUTINE A (Mon/Wed/Fri or every other day)
Curl-up, side plank, bird-dog, glute bridge, clamshell, hip flexor stretch, piriformis stretch
Time: 15-20 min

ROUTINE B (Tue/Thu or 2-3x/week)
Wall push-up, chair squat, band row, glute bridge
Time: 10-15 min

Can combine both into one 25-30 min session

MCGILL BIG 3 REP SCHEME (descending pyramid)
Set 1: 6 reps, Set 2: 4 reps, Set 3: 2 reps
Hold 6-8 sec each

OTHER EXERCISES
3 sets prescribed reps
Rest 30-60 sec between sets

STRETCHES
2 holds 30 sec each side
Do at end when muscles warm

PROGRESSION

McGill Big 3:
Week 1-2: 6/4/2 reps, 6 sec holds
Week 3-4: 8/6/4 reps, 8 sec holds
Week 5-6: 10/8/6 reps, 10 sec holds

Hip:
Week 1-2: Glute bridges 3x8, clamshells 3x10
Week 3-4: Glute bridges 3x10, clamshells 3x12
Week 5-6: Glute bridges 3x12 (add 3 sec hold), clamshells 3x15 or add band

Resistance:
Week 1-2: 2 sets each (learn form)
Week 3-4: 3 sets low end rep range
Week 5-6: 3 sets, increase reps or harder variation
Week 7+: Progress variations (counter push-ups, deeper squats, heavier band)

AVOID
Sit-ups/crunches (repeated spinal flexion under load)
Superman (excessive spinal extension)
Russian twists (rotational load on flexed spine)
Heavy barbell work (excessive spinal compression)
Any exercise causing radiating leg pain
Holding breath
Pushing through hip pain

WHEN TO DO
Wait 30 min after waking (discs most hydrated, give time to settle)
Before or after walk
Stretches best after activity when muscles warm
NOT when fatigued or during sciatica flare
