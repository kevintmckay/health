SLEEP PLAN TEMPLATE
=====================

Customize this file with your own sleep goals.
Place your personal version in the personal/ folder.

SCHEDULE
---------
- Pick a wake time and keep it consistent 7 days/week
- Work backward 8 hours for your bedtime
- Even on weekends — consistency is key

MORNING ROUTINE
-----------------
- 10 min daylight within 30 min of waking
- Resets circadian rhythm for better sleep
- Overcast days still count

WIND-DOWN (30-60 min before bed)
----------------------------------
- Dim lights throughout the house
- Screens off or use night mode
- No news, social media, or stressful content
- Light reading, stretching, or breathing exercises

ENVIRONMENT
------------
- Room temperature: 65-68 F (18-20 C)
- Blackout dark — no light sources
- Pillow positioning for your sleep style
- No phone within arm's reach
