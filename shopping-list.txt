CRITICAL REMINDERS WHEN SHOPPING
=================================

PRE-PORTION IMMEDIATELY:
------------------------
✓ CHEESE: Cut into 1 oz pieces, wrap individually, label "1 serving"
✓ NUTS: Portion into 1/4 cup (1 oz) bags, label "1 serving"
✓ Put portioned items in front of fridge/pantry (easy access)
✓ Put bulk items in back (out of reach)


AVOID THESE:
------------
✗ Seed/vegetable oils (canola, soybean, corn oil)
✗ White bread, white pasta, white rice
✗ Processed snacks (chips, crackers, cookies)
✗ Sugary drinks (soda, juice, sweet tea)
✗ Pre-shredded cheese (has fillers)
✗ Flavored yogurt (added sugar - buy plain)
✗ Honey-roasted or flavored nuts (added sugar/oils)


READ LABELS:
------------
✓ Greek yogurt: Plain, full-fat, no added sugar
✓ Cottage cheese: 4% milkfat
✓ Canned fish: In water, not oil
✓ Bread: Whole grain, sprouted, or sourdough (not white)
✓ Nuts: Raw or dry roasted, unsalted
✓ Cheese: Real cheese, not "cheese product"
