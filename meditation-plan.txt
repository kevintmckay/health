MEDITATION PLAN TEMPLATE
==========================

Customize this file with your own practice goals.
Place your personal version in the personal/ folder.

GETTING STARTED
-----------------
- Start at 3 minutes per day
- Add 1 minute per week
- Target: 10-15 minutes by week 6
- Before phone/email in the morning

HOW TO PRACTICE
-----------------
1. Sit comfortably, close eyes
2. Focus on breath — in through nose, out through mouth
3. When mind wanders (it will), gently return to breath
4. That moment of noticing IS the practice

BREATHING TECHNIQUES
---------------------
4-7-8 Breathing (for anxiety):
- Inhale through nose for 4 counts
- Hold for 7 counts
- Exhale through mouth for 8 counts
- Repeat 3-4 cycles

RAIN Technique (for urges):
- Recognize what is happening
- Allow the experience to be there
- Investigate with kindness
- Non-identify (this feeling is not "me")
