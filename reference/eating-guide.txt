SIMPLIFIED EATING SYSTEM - FOOLPROOF GUIDE
===========================================

Daily targets: 120-140g protein, 50-70g fat, 3-4 cups vegetables, 2000 cal
For: Weight loss, anti-inflammatory eating, stress management


YOUR DAILY EATING PATTERN
==========================

BREAKFAST (30g protein minimum)
--------------------------------
Pick ONE every day, eat same thing for 2 weeks:

Option 1: 3 eggs + 1/2 cup Greek yogurt (30g)
Option 2: 2 eggs + 1 cup Greek yogurt (32g)
Option 3: 1 cup cottage cheese + 2 eggs (40g)
Option 4: 1.5 cups Greek yogurt + berries (35g)

No decisions. Same breakfast every day.


LUNCH (35-40g protein)
----------------------
YOUR LENTIL SAUTÉ (10-12 min):

Base (always the same):
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Onions + kale (sauté 5-7 min)
- 1 cup lentils (canned, drained) = 18g protein

Add ONE protein (rotate):
- 3 oz chicken (pre-cooked) = 21g - MOST DAYS
- 1/2 block tofu = 20g - 2-3x/week
- 2 eggs = 12g - occasionally

Top with: 1 oz feta (pre-portioned) = 4g protein

Total: 39-43g protein, fills you up, anti-inflammatory


DINNER (40-50g protein)
-----------------------
Simple plate formula:

Half plate = vegetables (2 cups, pre-roasted from Sunday batch)
Quarter plate = protein (palm-sized, 4-5 oz)
Quarter plate = 1 fist beans/lentils OR 1 potato/sweet potato

Protein options (rotate):
- Salmon 4-5 oz = 25-30g (2-3x/week - PRIORITY for omega-3s)
- Chicken 4-5 oz = 28-35g
- Burger patty 4 oz = 25-30g
- Tofu 1/2 block = 20g

Beans/lentils add: 15g protein (if using as starch)

Cook protein in air fryer while reheating vegetables = 12-15 min


SNACKS (only if physically hungry)
-----------------------------------
Unlimited stress-safe foods:
- Raw carrots (pre-cut)
- Raw celery (pre-cut)
- Raw bell pepper strips

Protein snacks (if actually hungry):
- 1 string cheese = 6g
- 2 hard boiled eggs = 12g
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt = 10g
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese = 14g


KITCHEN CLOSES 8 PM
-------------------
Brush teeth after dinner
No food after 8 PM (water, tea only)

If genuinely hungry before bed (not boredom/stress):
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese = 14g protein
- Use CBT tools to check if truly hungry vs. emotional


ANTI-INFLAMMATORY EATING (CRITICAL FOR SCIATICA)
=================================================

With chronic sciatica and hip issues, what you eat affects your pain levels.

PRIORITIZE DAILY:
-----------------
- Fatty fish 2-3x/week (salmon, sardines) - omega-3s directly reduce inflammation
- Colorful vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, beets) - antioxidants
- Extra virgin olive oil - primary cooking fat
- Nuts (walnuts, almonds) - but watch portions (1/4 cup max)
- Turmeric and ginger - add to lentil sauté, eggs, vegetables

REDUCE/AVOID:
-------------
- Seed oils (canola, vegetable, soybean) - pro-inflammatory
- Fried foods
- Processed meats as daily staple
- Excess sugar and refined carbs
- Highly processed packaged snacks
- Alcohol (disrupts sleep, increases next-day anxiety, adds empty calories)


FIBER (PREVENT CONSTIPATION)
=============================

High protein (120-140g) + low fiber = constipation disaster

TARGET: 25-30g fiber per day

YOU GET FIBER FROM:
-------------------
- Beans/lentils (1 cup = 15g fiber) - eating daily ✓
- Vegetables (4 cups = 10-12g fiber) - eating daily ✓
- Berries, sweet potatoes, oats
- Total: 25-30g easily if following the plan ✓

IF CONSTIPATED DESPITE THIS:
-----------------------------
- Drink MORE water (100-120 oz critical with high protein)
- Add fiber supplement (psyllium husk) 5-10g/day
- Increase magnesium to 400mg before bed
- More fiber = need even more water


THE SIMPLE RULES
================

PROTEIN (every meal):
---------------------
- Breakfast: 30g minimum (3 eggs + yogurt OR cottage cheese + eggs)
- Lunch: 35-40g (lentils + protein + feta)
- Dinner: 40-50g (4-5 oz protein + beans/lentils if using)
- Snacks (if needed): 10-20g
- Target: 130-150g/day total

Why 30g at breakfast: Research shows older adults (50+) need minimum 30g protein
per meal to maximize muscle protein synthesis and prevent muscle loss during weight loss.

Limits:
- Eggs: 21-28 per week maximum (3-4 per day)
- Tuna: 2-3 cans per week maximum (mercury)
- Solid cheese: 3 oz/day maximum (not including cottage cheese)

VEGETABLES (half your plate):
------------------------------
- Lunch: 1.5 cups (kale in sauté + roasted veg on side)
- Dinner: 2 cups (pre-roasted from Sunday)
- Snacks: Unlimited raw carrots/celery/bell pepper
- Target: 3-4 cups/day total

CHEESE (easy to overeat - watch this):
---------------------------------------
- Maximum 3 oz solid cheese/day (NOT including cottage cheese)
- Example: 1 oz feta + 1 oz swiss + 1 string cheese = 3 oz STOP
- Cottage cheese: for breakfast or if genuinely hungry (not unlimited)
- Pre-portion everything into 1 oz pieces

NUTS (easy to overeat - watch this):
-------------------------------------
- Maximum 1/4 cup per day (14 walnuts OR 23 almonds)
- Pre-portion into 1/4 cup bags
- Never eat from large bag
- Pair with meals, not alone

FATS/OILS:
----------
- Cook with: Extra virgin olive oil (1 tbsp per meal)
- High heat: Avocado oil (air fryer)
- AVOID: Seed oils (canola, vegetable, soybean)
- Omega-3: Eat salmon 2-3x/week OR take fish oil supplement

CARBS/STARCHES:
---------------
- Beans/lentils = your main starch (1 cup = 1 fist)
- If no beans: 1 fist rice OR 1 potato OR 2 slices whole grain bread
- Maximum 2-3 fists starch per day
- Sweet potatoes > white potatoes (anti-inflammatory)

WATER:
------
- 100-120 oz per day (high protein requires it)
- Carry water bottle, refill 3-4x daily
- Taper after 6 PM (prevent nighttime bathroom trips)


BATCH PREP SUNDAY (45 MIN)
===========================

Do all 4:

1. INSTANT POT (12 min):
   - Cook chicken thighs (4-5 lbs raw)
   - Shred/portion into 3-4 oz containers
   - Lasts all week

2. STOVETOP (10 min):
   - Hard boil 10-12 eggs
   - Peel when cool, store in container

3. AIR FRYER (30 min, 3 batches @ 400°F):
   - Batch 1: Cabbage + onions (15 min)
   - Batch 2: Bell peppers (12 min)
   - Batch 3: Brussels sprouts (15 min)
   - Toss each with 1 tbsp olive oil + salt
   - Store in containers

QUICK COOK TIMES (Air fryer 400°F):
- Salmon: 10-12 min
- Tofu cubes: 12-15 min
- Burger patties (frozen): 8-10 min
- Shrimp (frozen): 6-8 min

4. RAW VEGETABLES (10 min):
   - Cut carrots into sticks → container with water
   - Cut celery into sticks → container with water
   - Slice bell peppers → container (no water)
   - Chop kale → bag with paper towel

RESULT: Zero cooking Mon-Fri, just grab and heat


PRE-PORTIONING (DO THIS OR YOU'LL OVEREAT)
===========================================

WHEN YOU GET HOME FROM STORE:

Cheese (all of it):
-------------------
- Cut feta into 1 oz portions (2 tbsp crumbled)
- Cut swiss into 1 oz pieces (thumb-sized)
- Wrap each piece individually
- Label "1 oz serving"
- Put in front of fridge
- Take ONE piece per meal

Nuts (all of it):
-----------------
- Scoop 1/4 cup into small bags
- Label "1 oz serving"
- Make 14 bags (2 weeks worth)
- Put in front of pantry
- Take ONE bag per day maximum

Chicken (after cooking):
------------------------
- Portion into 3-4 oz containers
- Label each container
- Makes it easy to grab correct amount

DO NOT SKIP PRE-PORTIONING
You will overeat cheese and nuts otherwise


STRESS EATING DEFENSE SYSTEM
=============================

NEVER EAT STANDING UP
---------------------
If you're going to eat:
- Must put it on a plate
- Must sit at table
- Put phone/laptop away
- This 10-second pause stops most stress eating

AFTERNOON (HIGHEST RISK: 2-5 PM)
---------------------------------
When urge hits:
1. Set 10-minute timer
2. Drink full glass of water
3. Grab pre-cut carrots or celery (crunch helps)
4. Walk around house or to mailbox
5. When timer goes off, check if still hungry
6. If yes: eat planned snack (protein)
7. If no: crisis averted

EVENING RULE
------------
Kitchen closes 8 PM
Brush teeth immediately after dinner
If urge hits after 8 PM:
- Hot tea (no calories)
- 4-7-8 breathing (4 sec in, 7 sec hold, 8 sec out, repeat 4x)
- Go to bed early (better than stress eating)

UNLIMITED STRESS FOODS (KEEP VISIBLE)
--------------------------------------
Front of fridge, always available:
- Carrots (pre-cut)
- Celery (pre-cut)
- Bell pepper strips

These are FREE. Eat unlimited. Zero guilt.

TROUBLESHOOTING
===============

"I'M STILL HUNGRY"
------------------
Add more:
- Vegetables (unlimited)
- Protein snack
- Water (often thirst, not hunger)

Don't add:
- More cheese (calorie bomb)
- More nuts (calorie bomb)
- Starches (won't fill you up)

"I MESSED UP AND ATE OFF-PLAN"
-------------------------------
- One meal doesn't ruin anything
- Don't restrict next meal to "make up for it"
- Just eat your next planned meal normally
- Log it in food/mood journal (what triggered it?)
- Move on

"I'M TRAVELING / SCHEDULE DISRUPTED"
-------------------------------------
Minimal viable routine:
- Breakfast: Eggs or Greek yogurt (hotel, grocery store)
- Lunch: Rotisserie chicken + bag of carrots
- Dinner: Grilled protein + vegetables (any restaurant)
- Snacks: String cheese, hard boiled eggs (gas station)
- Skip fancy meals, focus on protein + vegetables

"I DON'T HAVE TIME TO PREP SUNDAY"
-----------------------------------
Bare minimum (15 min):
- Buy rotisserie chickens (pre-cooked)
- Hard boil eggs (10 min)
- Cut carrots/celery (5 min)
- Use frozen vegetables (zero prep)
This works. Not ideal, but prevents failure.

"I'M CONSTIPATED"
-----------------
Check:
- Drinking 100-120 oz water? (most common cause)
- Eating beans/lentils daily? (fiber)
- Eating vegetables daily? (fiber)
If all yes and still constipated:
- Add fiber supplement (psyllium husk)
- Increase magnesium dose to 400mg
