7-DAY MEAL PLAN
===============

Research-based targets per day:
- Protein: 120-140g (1.2-1.5 g/kg goal body weight for men 50+ during weight loss)
- Fiber: 25-30g (prevents constipation on high protein)
- Vegetables: 3-4 cups
- Omega-3: 2-3 servings fatty fish per week
- Calories: ~2000 (for weight loss)


MONDAY (125g protein, 30g fiber)
---------------------------------
Breakfast:
- 2 eggs scrambled = 12g protein
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt plain = 10g protein
TOTAL: 22g

Lunch:
- 1 cup lentils cooked = 18g protein, 15g fiber
- 3 oz chicken thigh = 21g protein
- 1 cup kale sautéed in 1 tbsp olive oil = 3g fiber
- 1 oz feta crumbled = 4g protein
TOTAL: 43g

Snack (afternoon):
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt = 10g protein

Dinner:
- 5 oz chicken thigh = 35g protein
- 2 cups roasted vegetables (cabbage, bell peppers) = 6g fiber
- 1 medium sweet potato = 2g protein, 4g fiber
TOTAL: 37g

Snack (if needed):
- 2 hard boiled eggs = 12g protein

DAILY TOTAL: 124g protein, 28g fiber, 4 cups vegetables

FIBER SOURCES:

- Vegetables = 2-3g per cup (varies)
- Sweet potato = 4g per medium

VEGETABLES counted toward daily total:

- Salad greens counted as 2 cups


PRACTICAL TIPS
==============

Water: Drink 100-120 oz daily (critical with high protein/fiber)

Batch prep Sunday:
- Cook all chicken (Instant Pot 12 min)
- Hard boil 10-12 eggs
- Roast vegetables (air fryer)
- Portion chicken into 3-4 oz containers

Snacks are flexible:
- Choose based on daily protein total
- If breakfast/lunch/dinner already hit 120g, skip snacks
- If below 120g, add 1-2 protein snacks

Kitchen closes: 8 PM (brush teeth after dinner)
- Friday includes optional evening snack if genuinely hungry before bed
- Use CBT tools to verify true hunger vs. emotional eating

Substitutions allowed:
- Greek yogurt ↔ cottage cheese
- Chicken ↔ turkey
- Any vegetables (keep 2 cups/meal)
- Lentils ↔ black beans ↔ chickpeas
- Salmon ↔ sardines (both high omega-3)
