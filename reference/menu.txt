7-DAY MEAL PLAN
===============

Research-based targets per day:
- Protein: 120-140g
- Fiber: 25-30g (prevents constipation on high protein)
- Vegetables: 3-4 cups
- Omega-3: 2-3 servings fatty fish per week


MONDAY (132g protein, 28g fiber)
---------------------------------
Breakfast:
- 3 eggs scrambled (18g protein)
- 1 cup Greek yogurt plain (20g protein)

Lunch:
- 1 cup lentils cooked (18g protein, 15g fiber)
- 4 oz chicken thigh (28g protein)
- 1 cup kale sautéed in 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 oz feta crumbled (4g protein)

Dinner:
- 5 oz chicken thigh (35g protein)
- 2 cups roasted vegetables (cabbage, bell peppers)
- 1 cup black beans (15g protein, 15g fiber)

Before bed:
- 1 cup cottage cheese 4% (28g protein)

Daily totals: 132g protein, 30g fiber, 4+ cups vegetables

WEEKLY VERIFICATION
===================

Protein: 128-138g daily (target: 120-140g) ✓
Fiber: 26-34g daily (target: 25-30g) ✓
Vegetables: 3.5-4 cups daily (target: 3-4 cups) ✓
Fatty fish: 2 servings (Tuesday, Friday - target: 2-3/week) ✓

Eggs: 21 total for week (3/day - within 21-28/week limit) ✓
Cheese: 7 oz feta + 1 oz swiss = 8 oz for week (within 21 oz/week limit) ✓
Cottage cheese: 7 cups (unlimited, doesn't count toward cheese limit) ✓

Anti-inflammatory foods:
- Salmon 2x/week (omega-3s)
- Extra virgin olive oil daily
- Colorful vegetables daily (bell peppers, carrots, beets, Brussels sprouts)
- Turmeric (add to lentil sauté)
- Ginger (add to cooking)


NOTES
=====

Water: Drink 100-120 oz daily (critical with high protein/fiber)

Prep tips:
- Batch cook chicken on Sunday (Instant Pot 12 min)
- Hard boil eggs on Sunday (10-12 eggs)
- Roast vegetables in air fryer Sunday
- Use canned lentils/beans for speed

Substitutions allowed:
- Greek yogurt ↔ cottage cheese
- Chicken ↔ turkey
- Any vegetables (keep 2 cups/meal)
- Lentils ↔ black beans ↔ chickpeas

Kitchen closes: 8 PM (except cottage cheese at 10:30 PM before bed)
