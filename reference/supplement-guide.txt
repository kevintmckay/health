SUPPLEMENT GUIDE
================

GET BLOODWORK FIRST
Request: Vitamin D, B12, lipids, iron, A1C, TSH


TAKE THESE (START THIS WEEK)
=============================

1. VITAMIN D3
-------------
Dose: 2000 IU daily
When: Morning with breakfast (needs fat for absorption)
Form: D3 softgel (NOT D2)
Cost: $10-15 for 3-6 months
Brands: NatureWise, NOW Foods, Thorne

Target blood level: 40-60 ng/mL
Recheck in 8-12 weeks


2. MAGNESIUM GLYCINATE
----------------------
Dose: Start 200mg before bed, increase to 400mg after 2 weeks
When: 1-2 hours before bed (with or without food)
Form: Glycinate (NOT oxide)
Cost: $15-20 for 2-3 months
Brands: Doctor's Best, KAL, Pure Encapsulations

Note: Reduces to 200mg if loose stools occur


3. OMEGA-3 FISH OIL
-------------------
Dose: 2-3g EPA+DHA per day
When: With dinner on non-fish days
When to SKIP: If you ate salmon/sardines that day
Form: Triglyceride form, third-party tested (IFOS, USP)
Cost: $20-40/month
Brands: Nordic Naturals, Carlson, Viva Naturals
Storage: REFRIGERATE after opening

Read label: "EPA 1200mg + DHA 800mg" = 2000mg total
Not total fish oil - look for combined EPA+DHA amount


4. CALCIUM CITRATE
------------------
Dose: 500mg daily
When: Morning with breakfast (alongside Vitamin D)
Form: Calcium citrate (NOT carbonate - better absorbed, less constipation)
Cost: $10-15 for 3-4 months
Brands: Citracal, NOW Foods, Nature Made

Why you need this:
- Men 51-70 need 1000mg calcium/day
- Your diet provides only 500-630mg/day
- Age 57 + weight loss = bone loss risk
- 500mg supplement fills the gap without over-supplementing

Note: Take separately from iron/zinc supplements (if taking) - calcium blocks absorption


DAILY SCHEDULE
==============

MORNING (with breakfast):
- Vitamin D3: 2000 IU
- Calcium citrate: 500mg

EVENING (1-2 hours before bed):
- Magnesium glycinate: 200-400mg

WITH DINNER (on non-fish days only):
- Omega-3 fish oil: 2-3g EPA+DHA

TOTAL COST: $55-90 upfront, lasts 2-3 months


ADD LATER (OPTIONAL)
====================

4. CREATINE MONOHYDRATE
-----------------------
Dose: 5g daily (anytime)
When to start: Week 4-6 after starting resistance exercises
Cost: $15-20 for 6+ months
Brands: Optimum Nutrition, Bulk Supplements
Side effect: 2-4 lbs water retention (normal)


ONLY IF NEEDED
==============

FIBER SUPPLEMENT (psyllium husk):
- Only if constipated despite eating beans/vegetables daily
- 5-10g daily with 16 oz water
- Take 2+ hours away from other supplements
- Cost: $10-15 for 1-2 months

PEA PROTEIN POWDER (you have this):
- Use as backup only on days you fall short on food protein
- 0-2 scoops max

VITAMIN B12:
- Only if bloodwork shows deficiency
- You eat eggs/chicken/yogurt daily - likely NOT deficient
- 500-1000 mcg daily if needed
- Cost: $10 for 6+ months


DON'T WASTE MONEY ON
====================

- Multivitamins
- Testosterone boosters
- Fat burners/thermogenics
- Collagen supplements
- BCAAs (you eat 120g protein/day)
- Pre-workout supplements
- Probiotics (eat yogurt/sauerkraut instead)
- Glucosamine/chondroitin (weak evidence)
- Turmeric supplements (add powder to cooking instead)


BLOODWORK TO REQUEST
====================

□ Vitamin D (25-hydroxyvitamin D)
□ Vitamin B12
□ Iron panel (ferritin)
□ Lipid panel (LDL, HDL, triglycerides)
□ HbA1c (blood sugar)
□ TSH (thyroid)
□ Testosterone (if fatigue/low energy)

Adjust supplements based on results


BUYING TIPS
===========

Look for:
- Third-party tested (USP, NSF, ConsumerLab, IFOS for fish oil)
- Avoid proprietary blends
- Check expiration dates

Where to buy:
- Amazon, Costco, iHerb, local health food store
- Avoid gas stations, dollar stores
