DAILY SCHEDULE TEMPLATE
========================

Customize this file with your own daily routine.
Place your personal version in the personal/ folder.

MORNING
--------
- Wake at consistent time
- 10 min daylight exposure within 30 min of waking
- 3-15 min meditation before checking phone/email
- Breakfast following your diet plan

MIDDAY
-------
- Scheduled walk or exercise
- Planned meals with proper portions
- CBT check-in: pause before eating, notice feelings

EVENING
--------
- Wind-down routine 30-60 min before bed
- Kitchen closes at set time
- Dim lights, screens off
- Consistent bedtime
