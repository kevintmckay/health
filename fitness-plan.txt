FITNESS PLAN TEMPLATE
=======================

Customize this file with your own fitness goals.
Place your personal version in the personal/ folder.

OVERVIEW
---------
- Walking is the primary starting intervention
- Progress gradually — add 2-5 min per week
- High protein intake supports muscle preservation
- Pool exercise is excellent for low-impact training

PROGRESSION
------------
Week 1-2:  Walking only, short sessions
Week 3-4:  Add core stability exercises
Week 5-6:  Add resistance training
Week 7+:   Add pool/aquatic exercise

KEY PRINCIPLES
---------------
- Never increase more than 10% per week
- Stop if sharp pain or radiating symptoms
- Consistency matters more than intensity
- Track your sessions to stay accountable
