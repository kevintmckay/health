FLARE-UP PROTOCOL TEMPLATE
============================

Customize this file with your own protocol.
Place your personal version in the personal/ folder.

MILD FLARE-UP
---------------
- Reduce walk by 5-10 minutes
- Add standing back extensions 3-4x per day
- Continue core exercises at reduced intensity
- Ice or heat for 15 minutes as needed

MODERATE FLARE-UP
-------------------
- Shorten walk to 5-10 minutes only
- Pause resistance exercises
- Continue gentle core work if tolerable
- Ice/heat 15 min, alternate as needed
- Change positions every 20 minutes

SEVERE FLARE-UP
-----------------
- Pause walking and exercises
- Use 90-90 position for relief (legs on chair)
- Change positions every 20 minutes
- Focus on breathing and gentle movement
- Contact your healthcare provider if needed

RETURNING TO ACTIVITY
-----------------------
- Resume at previous week's intensity (not from scratch)
- Rebuild over 3-5 days
- Do not jump back to where you were before the flare
- Listen to your body — mild discomfort OK, sharp pain is not
