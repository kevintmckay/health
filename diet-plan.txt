DIET PLAN TEMPLATE
====================

Customize this file with your own dietary goals.
Place your personal version in the personal/ folder.

CORE PRINCIPLES
-----------------
- Palm-sized protein at every meal
- Half plate of vegetables
- Fist-sized portion of starch/carbs
- Ramp protein gradually to avoid digestive issues

WEEKLY TARGETS
---------------
- Fatty fish 2-3x per week
- Cut liquid calories (soda, juice, alcohol)
- Track food for first 5 days to calibrate portions
- Then switch to palm/fist portion system

HYDRATION
----------
- 3-4 bottles of water daily
- One bottle first thing in the morning
- Spread evenly throughout the day
