ROUTINE A — CORE & HIPS (M/W/F)
----------------------------------
1. Modified curl-up — 6/4/2 reps, 6-8 sec holds
2. Side plank — 6/4/2 reps each side, 6-8 sec holds
3. Bird-dog — 6/4/2 reps each side, 6-8 sec holds
4. Hip flexor stretch — 30 sec each side
5. Piriformis stretch — 30 sec each side

ROUTINE B — RESISTANCE (Tu/Th)
---------------------------------
1. Wall push-ups — 2 sets of 10
2. Chair squats — 2 sets of 8
3. Resistance band rows — 2 sets of 10

PRINCIPLES
-----------
- Descending pyramid: 6 reps > 4 reps > 2 reps
- Hold each rep 6-8 seconds
- Stretches at the end when muscles are warm
- Progress by adding reps or hold time, not speed
