  DAILY TARGET

  PROTEIN SOURCES & PORTIONS

  EGGS

  - Per serving: 1 egg = 6g protein
  - Daily limit: 3-4 eggs (18-24g)
  - Weekly limit: 21-28 eggs
  - Uses: Breakfast, lentil sauté, snacks

  ---
  CHICKEN THIGHS

  - Per serving: 4-5 oz cooked = 28-35g protein
  - Daily amount: 4-10 oz (at 1-2 meals)
  - Weekly amount: 50-70 oz (4-5 lbs raw)
  - Prep: Instant Pot 12 min, Air fryer 18-20 min
  - Uses: Lentil sauté, dinner, batch prep Sunday

  DAILY MEAL STRUCTURE

  BREAKFAST (25-35g protein):
  - 2-3 eggs + Greek yogurt OR
  - 2-3 eggs + 1 oz swiss OR
  - Greek yogurt + 2 tbsp feta OR
  - Cottage cheese (1 cup)

  LUNCH (30-40g protein):
  - Lentil sauté: 4 oz chicken + 1 cup lentils + 1 oz feta OR
  - Lentil sauté: 1/2 block tofu + 1 cup lentils OR
  - Lentil sauté: 3 eggs + 1 cup lentils

  DINNER (40-50g protein):
  - 5-6 oz chicken + beans/lentils + vegetables OR
  - 5-6 oz salmon + vegetables + 2 tbsp parmesan OR
  - Burger patty + beans + vegetables

  SNACK (10-20g, if needed):
  - Greek yogurt OR
  - 1-2 hard boiled eggs OR
  - 1 string cheese + carrots OR
  - Cottage cheese (1/2 cup)

  BEFORE BED:
  - 1 cup cottage cheese (4%) = 28g protein

  DAILY TOTAL: 120-140g protein

  ---
  PREP SUNDAY (2 HOURS)

  1. Instant Pot: Cook all chicken thighs (12 min), shred/portion
  2. Stovetop: Hard boil 10-12 eggs
  3. Air fryer: Roast vegetables (bell pepper, onion, cabbage)
  4. Portion cheese: Cut all blocks into 1 oz pieces, wrap individually
  5. Done: Grab-and-go meals all week
