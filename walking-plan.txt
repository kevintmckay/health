WALKING PLAN TEMPLATE
=======================

Customize this file with your own walking goals.
Place your personal version in the personal/ folder.

GETTING STARTED
-----------------
- Week 1: 4 walks of 10 min each on flat surfaces
- Add 2-5 min per week progressively
- Target: 30-45 min walks by week 8-9

GUIDELINES
-----------
- Same time daily builds habit fastest
- Flat, even surfaces to start
- Comfortable supportive shoes
- Stop if sharp or radiating pain
- If pain flares, shorten next walk by 5 min

PROGRESSION
------------
Week 1:  4 x 10 min
Week 2:  4 x 12-15 min
Week 3:  4 x 15-20 min
Week 4:  4 x 20-25 min
Week 5+: Continue adding 2-5 min/week
